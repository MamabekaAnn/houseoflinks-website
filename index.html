About Us

Our Main/Parent Company is Links Prosols. House of Links is its Marketing, Management, and Communication division, overseeing the branches. We are a multi-service brand dedicated to providing convenient, reliable, and professional solutions for our community. We operate through three divisions, each built to support everyday needs and deliver high-quality services under one trusted name. The first division, Links Printing Solutions, focuses on digital, internet, and technology services. This includes internet café access, printing services, IT support, website development, and company registration assistance. The second division, Links Cleaning, provides dependable cleaning solutions for homes, businesses, vehicles, and sneaker care through Sneakers Links all aimed at providing fresh, clean, and professional results. Our mission is to provide convenience, reliability, and high-quality solutions across all services.
